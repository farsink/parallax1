INSIGHTS

Animal Planet is an American multinational pay television channel and associated website, owned by Warner Bros. Discovery Networks. Launched on June 1, 1996, the channel primarily focuses on series and documentaries about wild animals and domestic pets.

Key Features

  • Primarily devoted to animal-related content
  • Owned by Warner Bros. Discovery Networks
  • Features documentaries and series about wild animals and domestic pets
  • Has undergone branding changes, including a new logo and slogan “Same Planet, Different World”
